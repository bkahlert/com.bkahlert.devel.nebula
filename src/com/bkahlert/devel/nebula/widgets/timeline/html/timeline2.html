Timeline

An Eclipse Rich Client Platform (RCP) Wrapper for the SIMILE Timeline Widget

This widget allows you to integrate the SIMILE Timeline Widget in your Eclipse RCP application.

Simply instantiate a com.bkahlert.devel.nebula.widgets.timeline.impl.SelectionTimeline instance and call show(String json) on it.
